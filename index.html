About Me

A little more about who I am and what I enjoy.

I’m Alex Wanyoike, a detail-oriented creator with a love for elegant layout and calm color palettes. I enjoy transforming raw content into structured, readable pages that feel modern and accessible on any device. Outside design, I’m inspired by Kenyan cityscapes, thoughtful music, and nature photography.

My process is content-first: wireframe, typography, spacing scale, and contrast—followed by careful image choices. I’m comfortable with utility-style CSS, CSS Grid/Flexbox, and progressive enhancement.

I’m currently open to collaborations on landing pages, one-page resumes, and simple multi-section sites built with semantic HTML & CSS only.
